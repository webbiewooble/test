Our Philosophy

A Letter from Sampriti

"Sampriti Botanicals was born from a simple realization: that the wisdom of our ancestors is not a relic of the past, but a living, breathing guide for the present. Our journey began in the foothills of the Himalayas, where we sought out the last remaining practitioners of traditional herbalism."

"We believe that true wellness is a harmony of the physical, mental, and spiritual. Our formulations are not just products; they are rituals, designed to reconnect you with the natural world and your own inner rhythm."

"Every ingredient we use is ethically sourced, sustainably harvested, and processed with the utmost care. We are committed to transparency, quality, and the preservation of ancient knowledge."
