SHAKTI PEYA | Energy Elixir

Activation ⋅ Anti-Aging ⋅ Radiance

$33

Shakti Peya is an invigorating elixir meticulously formulated to awaken the body's dormant vitality. This potent synergy of time-honored adaptogenic herbs—including Ashwagandha, Shatavari, and Brahmi—works in harmonious concert to support adrenal health and fortify the nervous system against the stresses of modern life. Unlike traditional stimulants that offer a fleeting surge followed by a crash, Shakti Peya provides a clean, jitter-free activation, promoting sustained mental clarity and physical energy throughout the day. Infused with the delicate notes of Rose Petals and the warmth of Cardamom, it is crafted for those seeking a sophisticated ritual to cultivate a balanced, focused, and radiant state of being. Ideal for morning consumption or as a midday pick-me-up, it helps restore equilibrium and supports long-term resilience.

How to Use

Mix one test tube with hot water or milk of choice. Stir well and enjoy.

Ingredients

Ashwagandha, Shatavari, Brahmi, Tulsi, Rose Petals, Cardamom.
